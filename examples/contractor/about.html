About ForgeBuilt Exteriors

ForgeBuilt Exteriors is a local roofing and exterior company focused on honest service, clear communication, and high-quality workmanship. We help property owners across Southwest Washington protect their homes and buildings with durable systems built for long-term performance.

What We Believe

Your contractor should be reliable, transparent, and easy to work with. We show up on time, communicate clearly, and document every step of the project.

How We Work

Every job starts with a thorough inspection and a detailed scope. You’ll know exactly what’s needed, what it costs, and how long it takes before work begins.

Who We Serve

Homeowners, landlords, and businesses in Chehalis, Centralia, Olympia, Tumwater, Lacey, Rochester, Napavine, Winlock, and nearby areas.

Our Process

1) Inspect: On-site assessment and photos.
2) Plan: Scope, materials, and timeline.
3) Build: Skilled execution with clean jobsite standards.
4) Final Walkthrough: Quality check and closeout documentation.

Why Clients Refer Us

We don’t oversell. We fix what’s needed, stand behind our work, and make projects less stressful with dependable project communication.
